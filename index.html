About

I'm a geoinformatics student and GIS analyst specializing in spatial analysis, web mapping applications, and remote sensing. Currently pursuing an MSc in Geoinformatics and Cartography at Palacký University in Olomouc. I combine GIS technologies, spatial databases, and web development to create practical tools for geographic data analysis and visualization. My expertise spans QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, PostGIS, Python, and modern web mapping libraries like Leaflet and React.

  • GIS & Spatial Stack: QGIS, ArcGIS Pro, GDAL, PostGIS, Spatial SQL, GeoServer, MapServer, WMS/WFS/OGC services
  • Web GIS & Mapping: Leaflet, GeoJSON, REST APIs, web map services
  • Programming: Python, JavaScript, SQL, C#, HTML/CSS, Node.js
  • Databases: PostgreSQL, PostGIS, MySQL, database design & optimization
  • Computer Vision & AI: YOLO, OpenCV, video processing, AI model inference
  • Infrastructure & DevOps: Git/GitHub, Linux, Ubuntu Server, Docker, REST APIs

I'm also passionate about remote sensing, computer vision applications in GIS, and automation of geodata workflows. Looking to contribute to impactful projects in environmental monitoring, urban planning, and geospatial intelligence.
